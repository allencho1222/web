Research

Our mission is to advance the state-of-the-art of computing technology and improve the performance and efficiency of systems ranging from low-power edge devices to high-performance server systems. We pursue our goal through innovation and optimization across different levels, including computer architecture, runtime systems, compiler, and algorithm.
